## Action Required for Team Members

All CV PDF files in the `public/cv/` folder need to be updated to comply with US/Western standards for professional CVs. Use the ATS-compliant templates as a reference.

### Required Changes:

1. **Remove Photos** - Remove all photos from CVs. US CV standards do not include photos.

2. **Remove Personal Identifiable Information (PII)**:
   - Home addresses (location/country only)
   - Personal phone numbers (email contact only)
   - Any other sensitive personal information

3. **Keep Only Professional Information**:
   - Name
   - Professional title/role
   - Email address (professional)
   - LinkedIn profile (optional)
   - Professional experience
   - Skills and qualifications

4. **ATS Compliance Formatting**:
   - Simple, clean layout (no tables, graphics, or complex formatting)
   - Standard fonts (Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman)
   - Black text on white background
   - Standard section headers (PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY, EXPERIENCE, EDUCATION, etc.)
   - Chronological format with clear date ranges
