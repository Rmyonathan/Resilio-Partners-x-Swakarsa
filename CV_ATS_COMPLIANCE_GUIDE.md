# ATS-Compliant Resume Guide for Marketing Team

## Overview
All marketing team member resumes have been converted to US ATS (Applicant Tracking System) compliant formats. These resumes follow US professional standards and are optimized for automated resume screening systems.

## ATS Compliance Features Applied

### ✅ Removed from All Resumes:
- **Photos** - No profile pictures (US standard practice)
- **Home Addresses** - Only location (country) for privacy and security
- **Phone Numbers** - Removed personal phone numbers (optional professional contact only)

### ✅ Standard Formatting:
- **Clean, Simple Layout** - No complex tables, graphics, or formatting
- **Standard Section Headers** - PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY, TECHNICAL SKILLS, PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE, EDUCATION, CERTIFICATIONS
- **Keyword Optimized** - Industry-standard keywords and terminology
- **Chronological Format** - Clear date ranges for all experience
- **Consistent Formatting** - Uniform structure across all resumes

### ✅ Content Enhancements:
- **Professional Summaries** - Enhanced with relevant keywords
- **Quantifiable Achievements** - Added metrics and specific accomplishments where applicable
- **Action Verbs** - Used strong action verbs (Developed, Created, Managed, Led, etc.)
- **Relevant Skills** - Organized skills by category for easy ATS parsing

## Next Steps for Team Members

### To Create PDF Versions:
1. Open the `.txt` file in Microsoft Word or Google Docs
2. Apply minimal formatting:
   - Use standard fonts (Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman)
   - Font size: 10-12pt for body, 14-16pt for name
   - Black text on white background
   - Simple bullet points (no fancy symbols)
   - 1-inch margins
   - Single-column layout
3. Save as PDF
4. Test ATS compatibility using free tools like:
   - Jobscan.co
   - Resume.io ATS checker
   - VMock

### Formatting Tips:
- ✅ DO: Use simple bullet points (• or -)
- ✅ DO: Use standard date formats (Month YYYY - Month YYYY)
- ✅ DO: Keep formatting consistent
- ❌ DON'T: Use tables or columns
- ❌ DON'T: Use graphics, logos, or images
- ❌ DON'T: Use fancy fonts or colors
- ❌ DON'T: Use headers/footers with page numbers
- ❌ DON'T: Use text boxes or shapes

### File Naming Convention:
Recommended: `FirstName_LastName_Resume_ATS.pdf`

Example: `Ahmad_Musyaari_Rasyid_Resume_ATS.pdf`

## Security and Privacy Benefits

By removing personal identifiable information (PII):
- ✅ Reduced risk of identity theft
- ✅ Protection against stalking/harassment
- ✅ Compliance with US privacy best practices
- ✅ Professional appearance for Western clients
- ✅ ATS-friendly format for job applications

## Contact Information Standards
